Tuesday Night Rep Calculator

Build workouts where every pace group finishes at the same time.

Set your rep distance and recovery, then define pace groups with a range for each. The first group is your anchor — enter its rep count and all other groups are automatically calculated to finish in the same time. Editing a group's slowest pace will auto-update the next group's fastest so ranges always abut. Toggle any group to Manual to try a specific rep count and see the time impact vs. the anchor.
